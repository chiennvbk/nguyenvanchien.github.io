About

I'm a PMP®-certified Project Manager and Senior Process Engineer based in Vietnam, currently serving as Technical Manager at Mincore Pty Ltd (Australia). I bridge the gap between deep engineering expertise and project delivery — able to lead teams while speaking the language of every engineer on the floor.

My work spans gold refineries in Papua New Guinea, lithium scoping studies in Ghana, rare earth plants in Australia, and oil & gas FEED in the Netherlands. I bring process rigour (HYSYS, HAZOP, SIL) to project management, and project discipline to engineering work.

I hold a B.Eng. in Petrochemical Engineering from Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST) and the PMP® certification from PMI (No. 4159343, valid to 07/2028).
